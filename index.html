Our Story

The man behind the brand

👤
Founder & Creative Director
CEO Brand DZ · 16 years old · Algeria

I'm a 16-year-old Algerian guy, and I started CEO Brand out of a simple desire — I wanted to build something, to make money, to prove to myself that I could create a real business from zero. That was the spark.

But somewhere along the way, something changed. What started as a business move became a genuine passion. I fell in love with clothes, with fashion, with the way a design can say something without a single word. Now I don't just sell T-shirts — I think about every detail, every color, every line. The clothes became personal.

CEO is just the beginning. I want to build more — more brands, more ideas, more businesses. But right now, I'm focused on making CEO something real, something Algerians are proud to wear.

"My designs are my power. That's the one thing that's fully mine."

Started in Algeria. Built from scratch. Worn by the streets. — CEO Brand, 2026.
